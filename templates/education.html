ABHYASIKA (Free Tuition Center ) :

 
	ABHYASIKA is a Novel Project of Seva Bharati to
the Children living in Slums and not having the minimum
facilities and no encouragement from their parents after
coming back from the schools. And some children may not
even go to schools because the parents leave them in the
houses to look after the younger ones while they go on
work for their livelyhood. Sevabharati running 30
Abhyasikas at present in these localities. Since the children
are not taken care by the parents they become more
troublesome in the locality, finally they turn out to be
indiscipline, disadvantaged, deserted and involve in
juvenile delinquency. Hence the Abhyasika will take care
of such children and support them to keep them on right
path. 
				 
	This ABHYASIKA gives guidance, motivation and
support to the children in the age group of 9 to 14 for Two
hours a day and six days a week in their locality irrespective
of the school background.
				
	A Teacher will be appointed to train them properly.
The Teacher mingles with the children as well with the
parents and creates interest towards education. The
Teacher also gets training every month in the One Day
Training (Prasikshana Varga). Proper guidance will be given
to the Teacher for overall improvement of the child.
				



	Weekend tests will be conducted to the child to
evaluate the improvement with regard to script, verbal,
physical and mental abilities. Expert coaching will be given
to the children appearing for common exams.
